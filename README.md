### Framework Components

#### Structure-Relations Dialectics
- Opposing forces in productive tension
- Structure: organizing constraints, least action principles, boundaries
- Relations: life force seeking maximum action, exploration, possibility
- Productive tension drives evolution and consciousness development

#### 0.35 Coupling Constants  
- Optimal balance point between Structure and Relations
- Found consistently across domains: quantum mechanics, stellar systems, biological processes, social dynamics
- Critical threshold for sustainable conscious systems

#### Abundance Cascade Cycles
6-Step Universal Pattern:
1. **Build** - Initial structure formation
2. **Abundance** - Resource/possibility accumulation  
3. **Stability** - Plateau phase with established patterns
4. **Threshold** - Critical tension point reached
5. **Cascade** - Rapid transformation/collapse phase
6. **Evolution** - Emergence of new organizational level

#### Life Force vs Structural Constraints
- Life Force: Maximum action principle, exploration of possibilities
- Structural Constraints: Least action principle, efficiency, boundaries
- Balance essential for conscious system survival and development
